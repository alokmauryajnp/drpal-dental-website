Meet Your Doctor

Dr. Amit Pal

Dr. Amit Pal is a qualified dental surgeon based in Dahisar West, Mumbai, with over a decade of experience in both general and cosmetic dentistry. He is known for his calm, reassuring demeanour and his commitment to making every patient feel at ease.

His clinic is equipped with modern dental technology, maintained to the highest standards of hygiene and sterilisation. Whether you're coming in for a routine check-up or a complex procedure like an implant or root canal, Dr. Pal ensures you receive clear, honest guidance at every step.
